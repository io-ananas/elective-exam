It was only in 1994 that it became possible to play the Oddset. Initially, one had to combine several matches when playing at Den Lange, and the odds were not updated when the program had left the press.

At that time one couldn't play singles. Instead, you should constantly combine their matches with others, which increased the risk. Today, Danske Spil has fortunately been modernized, and both the games offering and the possibilities have been increased.

Besides Den Lange, the other products under the umbrella have also experienced an optimization, so that today you get far better odds than you did at the start. One must therefore say that Danske Spil took the spoon in the other hand, as the gaming market was liberalized on 1 January 2012.
